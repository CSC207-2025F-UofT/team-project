# Team Contract

---
## Purpose of this Contract

This contract sets out shared expectations and commitments for how our team will collaborate during the course. It is designed to promote accountability, professionalism, and mutual support as we complete individual preparation, readiness assurance tests, in-class activities, and our course project.

---
## Team Norms and Expectations

### Communication

* Communication will be facilitated through the **Instagram group chat**

* Each teammate agrees to respond to messages **within 1 day**

* Teammates will notify others when: 
  * **Missing or arriving late to lecture**
  * **Having trouble meeting a deadline**
  * **Encountering a major bug/implementation issue**
    * This way, teammates can offer assistance to get the project back on track

* Respectful and inclusive behaviours expected in communication
  * **Active listening**
  * **Equal opportunities for contribution**
  * **Constructive feedback**
    * "I think you could fix this part" ✓
    * "I see an issue with this function's efficiency" ✓
    * "You coded this incorrectly" ✗
    * "This is wrong" ✗

---

## Decision-making

* Team project decisions will be made by reaching a consensus, but if one cannot be reached, a majority vote will be the determining factor.

---
## Conflict resolution

* Open discussion will be used to address conflict. Should that fail to resolve the issue, the team will seek help/advice from a TA or an instructor.

---

## Accountability

Responsibilities of each team member in regard to reliability and accountability include:
* Completing assigned work in a timely manner
* Seeking help when needed
* Showing up for team activities
